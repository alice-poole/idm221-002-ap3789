THE GUILD NEEDS YOU

HOW TO JOIN


  1. Get a character sheet. Print out a physical sheet (available here) or build a character digitally via DnD Beyond.

  2. Build your character with any official books you want. If you are new, you can start for free with the basic rules or grab a pregenerated character.

  3. Log your character in the official Guild Log. This is a quick way to look up who you'll be playing with at any event. Welcome to the Guild!

  4. Check the schedule to RSVP for upcoming events. This helps match players with tables and lets the DM know how many players to expect.

  5. Join our official Discord for announcements, discussion, and digital events! Be sure to grab the D&D role for access to our exclusive channels.
