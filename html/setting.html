THE SWORD COAST

A LAND OF ADVENTURE


Welcome to the Sword Coast. To the West, the Sea of Swords sparkles in the evening sun. To the East, the dusty and dangerous Starmetal Hills guard the way to perilous deserts. To the North stands the proud city of Neverwinter, a hub of trade and power in the land. To the South, the icy Sword Mountains swallow up ancient ruins and monster-filled caves. In between is the Great Valley. This idyllic grassy plairie has been a frontier, a home, and a battlefield for generations. In the wake of the Skeleton War, one question is on everyone's minds: what's next for the Coast?
