LEILON

HOW TOUGH ARE YA?


Leilon's a gamble. Its citizens like it that way. On the one hand, it's a den of danger, evil, and monstrous folk. On the other hand, it's the best reprieve from the actual dens of dangerous evil monsters that litter the surrounding swamps.

This seaside town was loyal to the Skeleton Lord in the war. Makes sense, given the whole marsh full of undead. Even though the Pale Cult lost, word is some are still loyal to its cause.

Leilon locals are tough as nails: pirates, traders, adventurers, and thieves. The newly arrived group of royal Neverwinter settlers hoping to rebuild it will have to be tougher.


LOCATIONS


Umber Hulk's Shell Inn

The first inn built in the reclaimed town. This two-story wooden establishment is run by Alion Malwyn, the local gossip.

Knight's Goblet

A hotspot before the town's destruction, the Knight's Goblet has been rebuilt as a rest stop for merchants and wealthy travelers to spend the night. No weapons are permitted inside.

House of Thalivar

This half-crumbled wizard's tower used to watch over the town - literally. According to Neverwinter reports, it was turned into a giant magical weapon in the war.

Peculiarities Shoppe

Aubrey Silverspun runs this famous shop of trinkets and magic items, as she has for a century since her death. If you are lucky, this ghostly store might appear to you.

Quayside

This harbor hosts the Barge Yard and Fishery, where salty sailors do their work and try to ignore the smell. Some trade stories of unfathomable sea monsters and worse.
